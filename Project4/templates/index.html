A Convolutional Neural Network on:
Texas Fish Species Identification

Instructions for use:

The primary purpose of this convolutional neural network is to identify between 11 different species of game fish in the state of Texas. In order to utilize the neural network, select the "Choose File" button below and upload an image of a fish from your local device. Then select the "Predict" button and the neural network will display results of the predicted species of fish and confidence percentage as well as fishing regulations provided by the Texas Parks and Wildlife Department.
